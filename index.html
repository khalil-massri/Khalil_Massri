About

Dr. Khalil Massri is the Head of the Computer Science Department at the College of Information Technology, Hebron University. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from Sapienza University of Rome in 2014. His academic and research career focuses on the design and analysis of efficient algorithms and protocols for resource‑constrained, mobile, and delay‑tolerant networks.
